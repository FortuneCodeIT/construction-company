Frequently Asked Questions

How can I view my project progress?

You can view your project progress by clicking on the "Progress" tab in the sidebar. There you'll find a detailed timeline, completion percentage, and updates from your project manager.

How do I download my invoices?

Navigate to the "Budget" section in the sidebar. You'll find a complete list of all invoices. Click the "Download" button next to any invoice to save a PDF copy.

Can I make changes to my project?

To request changes to your project, please message your Project Manager directly from the "Messages" section. They will review your request and provide guidance on any additional costs or timeline adjustments.

How often are progress reports updated?

Progress reports are updated weekly, every Friday. Major milestones are updated in real-time. You'll receive email notifications when new reports are available.

How do I reset my password?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password". You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password. If you don't receive the email, please contact support.
